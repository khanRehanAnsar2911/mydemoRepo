About COVID-19

Getting Your Updated COVID-19 Vaccine(s)

1) Can I get More than one updated covid vaccine

Some people can get additional doses of COVID-19 vaccine: People aged 65 years and older may get a 2nd dose of updated Pfizer BioNTech or Moderna COVID-19 vaccine 4 or more months after the 1st updated COVID-19 vaccine. People who are moderately or severely immunocompromised may get additional doses of updated Pfizer BioNTech or Moderna COVID-19 vaccine 2 or more months after the last updated COVID-19 vaccine.
